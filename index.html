BRIEF BIOGRAPHY

I received his B.Sc., M.Sc., Ph.D. degrees all in computer science, from Central South University, China, in 2009, 2012 and 2016, respectively. Currently, I am an associate professor with the Department of Computer Science and Technology, Tsinghua University, Beijing, China. Prior to joining Tsinghua, I was a professor with the School of Computer Science and Engineering, Central South University, Changsha, China. My research interests include Internet-of-Things, edge computing, distributed & embedded AI, and operating system. I am a senior member of the IEEE and a member of the ACM. I was recognized as a highly cited researcher by Clarivate in 2021 and 2020.

I am looking for self-motivated Ph.D. students who are intrested in my research areas. Please feel free to contact me, if you want to join my group.
